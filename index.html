Biography

I am a Research Associate at the University of Luxembourg, working with Prof. Yves (IEEE Fellow) and Prof. Mike. I completed my PhD degree at the University of Luxembourg under the supervision of Prof. Yves (IEEE Fellow) and Prof. Mike. Before that, I received my Master's degree from King's College London, and my double B.S. degree from the Beijing University of Technology and University College Dublin.
